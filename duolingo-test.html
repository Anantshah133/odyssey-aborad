What is the Duolingo English Test ?

  • The Duolingo English Test (DET) is an online, computer-based English proficiency test for non-native speakers.
  • It evaluates the ability to use English in Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.
  • Recognized by over 4,000 institutions worldwide, it is widely used for university admissions and other purposes.

Benefits of the Duolingo English Test :-

  • Affordable : Costs significantly less than traditional English tests.
  • Convenient : Can be taken online from home.
  • Fast Results : Scores are available within 48 hours.
  • Widely Accepted : Recognized by thousands of institutions, including top universities in the USA, UK, and Canada
  • Adaptive Testing : Questions adjust based on your skill level.
  • Short Duration : The test takes only 1 hour to complete.

Duolingo English Test Eligibility :-

  • Age : No official age limit, but typically taken by candidates aged 13 and above. Those under 18 may need parental consent.
  • Education : No minimum educational qualification is required.
  • Purpose :-
    • Academic : For university admissions.
    • General Training : For work applications requiring proof of English proficiency.

Duolingo Exam Pattern

The Duolingo English Test consists of two main sections :

  • Adaptive Test (45 minutes) :
    • Skills Assessed : Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing..
    • Tasks Include :
      • Fill in the blanks.
      • Identify real English words from a list.
      • Listen to a sentence and type what you hear.
      • Describe an image.
      • Respond to a written prompt in 50 words.
  • Video Interview & Writing Sample (10 minutes) :
    • Video Interview : Answer open-ended questions via video..
    • Writing Sample : Write a short essay (50–100 words) on a given topic.
    • This section is shared with institutions for a holistic view of your language ability.

Duolingo English Test Syllabus

  • Reading :
    • Vocabulary and sentence completion.
    • Understanding written texts.
  • Listening :
    • Recognizing spoken words.
    • Understanding audio instructions or sentences.
  • Speaking :
    • Pronouncing sentences clearly.
    • Responding to prompts.
  • Writing :
    • Constructing meaningful sentences.
    • Writing essays or descriptions.

Marks Pattern

  • Scoring Range : 10–160, with a 5-point increment.
  • Proficiency Levels :
    • 155 - 160: Expert
    • 145 - 150: Advanced
    • 135 - 140: Upper-Intermediate
    • 115 - 130: Intermediate
    • Below 100: Basic to Low Proficiency

Mode of Exam

  • Online Test : Taken entirely online from the comfort of your home.
  • AI-Proctored : Uses AI to monitor test-takers and ensure integrity.

Preparation Tips and Tricks

  • Understand the Test Format : Familiarize yourself with the test's unique adaptive format.
  • Practice Daily :
    • Use Duolingo's practice test for a realistic experience.
    • Work on grammar, vocabulary, and listening comprehension.
  • Improve Typing Speed : Essential for quick responses.
  • Speak Clearly : Record yourself to assess fluency and pronunciation.
  • Time Management : Practice answering questions within the time limit.
  • Avoid Distractions :
    • Take the test in a quiet, well-lit room.
    • Avoid using external resources or looking away from the screen during the test.
  • Review Instructions : Read each question carefully to avoid mistakes
