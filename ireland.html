Higher Education Institutions in Ireland

  • Universities: Offer NFQ Levels 7-10, focusing on research and academic programs. There are a total of 11 government-funded universities providing undergraduate, postgraduate, and research degree programs.
  • Institutes of Technology: Practical, career-oriented courses at NFQ Levels 6-9. There are 14 government-funded institutes of technology offering a variety of programs that are both academically and vocationally focused.
  • Private Colleges: Specialized fields like business, IT, and arts at various NFQ levels. These mostly private colleges offer a range of programs including professional certificates, diplomas, undergraduate, and postgraduate degrees in a diverse range of subjects.

Why NFQ Matters?

  • Provides transparency and clarity for employers and institutions globally.
  • Facilitates progression routes, allowing students to move between levels seamlessly.
  • Ensures quality assurance across all qualifications.
